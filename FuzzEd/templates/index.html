Dependability Modeling Fault Trees and RBDs

Uncertainties in Modeling Introducing FuzzTrees

Our novel concept for fault tree modeling allows you to express configurable and uncertain parts of the system. We are working on an analysis engine that will tell you which configuration options give the best cost-dependability ratio.
