Dependability Modeling with Fault Trees and RBDs

If you need a quick way for drawing and analyzing your system's availability characteristics, this one is for you. We offer a free basic account so that you can just start.

Modeling with uncertainties - Introducing FuzzTrees

Our novel concept for fault tree modeling allows you to express configurable and uncertain parts of the system. Our analysis engine tells you which configuration options give the best cost-dependability ratio.

Graph sharing for teaching and collaboration

Graphs can be easily shared between users to support academic teaching and professional collaboration projects. We support the export as graphic, eMail attachment or project file.
